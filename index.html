Academy FAQs

Everything you need to know before enrolling.

Do I need an expensive camera to start Track A?
Not at all. We actually recommend starting with your smartphone. Track A focuses heavily on scripting, pacing, and storytelling—the elements that actually drive retention. We'll show you exactly how to upgrade your gear later when the channel starts paying for itself.
Is Track B right for me if I don't have a team yet?
Yes. Track B is specifically designed to help you build the backend systems so you can hire your first editor or manager. If you are drowning in editing and struggling to post consistently, this track provides the exact templates to start outsourcing.
How long do I have access to the materials?
You get lifetime access to the track you purchase. The YouTube algorithm changes constantly, so we update the curriculum quarterly. You will get all future updates for your track at no additional cost.

Agency FAQs

Common questions about working with our post-production team.

What is the typical turnaround time for a video?
For Tier 1 and Tier 2 edits, our standard turnaround is 4-5 business days from the moment you submit the raw footage and brief. Tier 3 full packages may take 7-10 days depending on the complexity of the pre-production planning. Rush delivery (48 hours) is available for an additional fee.
How do revisions work?
We use our integrated Client Portal (which features Frame.io style direct-video markup commenting) to make review cycles fast and direct. Tier 1 and Tier 2 packages include 1 full round of revisions, while Tier 3 includes up to 3 rounds.
Can I buy thumbnails without paying for video editing?
Absolutely. The Thumbnail Lab operates completely independently. You can hire us just for thumbnail design, just for editing, or bundle them together in Tier 3 for the ultimate hands-off workflow.
